About Our Gypsum Ceiling Tiles

Gypnova offers a wide range of premium gypsum ceiling tiles designed to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of any interior space. Our ceiling tiles combine elegant designs with excellent acoustic properties, fire resistance, and durability.

Each tile is manufactured using high-quality gypsum and advanced production techniques to ensure consistent quality, precise dimensions, and smooth finish. Whether you're looking for plain tiles for a minimalist look or decorative tiles for a more ornate ceiling, our collection has something to suit every style and requirement.

All our gypsum ceiling tiles are ISI certified, ensuring they meet the highest quality standards in the industry.

Installation Guide

Proper installation of gypsum ceiling tiles is essential to achieve the best results. Here's a brief overview of the installation process:

  1. Grid System Installation: Install a T-bar suspension system according to the manufacturer's instructions and local building codes.
  2. Measuring and Cutting: Measure the border areas and cut tiles to size using a utility knife or appropriate cutting tools.
  3. Tile Placement: Carefully place the tiles into the grid system, starting from the center of the room and working outward.
  4. Border Tiles: Install cut border tiles around the perimeter of the room.
  5. Finishing: Check that all tiles are properly seated in the grid and make any necessary adjustments.

For detailed installation instructions and best practices, please refer to our comprehensive installation guide or contact our technical support team.
