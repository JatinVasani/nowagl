About Gypnova

Leading manufacturer of premium gypsum products

Our Story

Gypnova is a leading manufacturer of premium gypsum products, dedicated to providing innovative building materials for modern construction. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the market.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Morbi, Gujarat, is equipped with the latest technology to produce high-quality gypsum boards, ceiling tiles, and powder products that meet international standards.

At Gypnova, we are committed to excellence in every aspect of our business, from product development to customer service. Our team of experts works tirelessly to ensure that our products exceed customer expectations and contribute to the success of construction projects across the country and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, innovative gypsum products that meet the evolving needs of the construction industry while maintaining the highest standards of customer service and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the leading manufacturer of gypsum products in India and a globally recognized brand known for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Core Values

Quality

We are committed to delivering products of the highest quality that exceed industry standards and customer expectations.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to develop advanced building materials that address the evolving needs of modern construction.

Sustainability

We are dedicated to environmentally responsible manufacturing practices and the development of eco-friendly products.

Customer Focus

We prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to build long-term relationships based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service.

Our Certifications

At Gypnova, we adhere to the highest quality standards and are proud to have received certifications from prestigious organizations. Our products are ISI certified, ensuring they meet the stringent quality requirements set by the Indian Standards Institute.

These certifications reflect our commitment to excellence and provide our customers with the assurance that they are investing in products of superior quality and reliability.

Our Global Presence

Gypnova has established a strong presence in the global market, exporting our premium gypsum products to countries across the world. Our commitment to quality and innovation has earned us the trust of international clients who rely on our products for their construction projects.

We continue to expand our global footprint, bringing the excellence of Gypnova products to new markets and contributing to the success of construction projects worldwide.
