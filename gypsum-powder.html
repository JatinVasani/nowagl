Gypnova offers a comprehensive range of premium gypsum powder products designed for various construction and industrial applications. Whether you need plaster of Paris for casting and molding, wall putty for smooth finishes, or jointing compounds for seamless drywall installations, we have the perfect solution for your specific requirements.

Application Guide

Proper application of gypsum powder products is essential to achieve the best results. Here's a brief overview of the general application process:

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free from dust, grease, and loose particles.
  2. Mixing: Add the powder to clean water as per the recommended ratio and mix thoroughly until a smooth, lump-free consistency is achieved.
  3. Application: Apply the mixed material using appropriate tools such as trowels, spatulas, or putty knives, depending on the specific product and application.
  4. Finishing: Smooth the surface as required and allow it to dry completely before sanding or applying subsequent coats.
  5. Curing: Allow the applied material to cure as per the recommended time before proceeding with further finishing or decoration.

For detailed application instructions and best practices for specific products, please refer to our comprehensive application guides or contact our technical support team.
