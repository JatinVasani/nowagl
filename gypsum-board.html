Installation Guide

Proper installation of gypsum boards is essential to achieve the best results. Here's a brief overview of the installation process:

  1. Preparation: Ensure the framing is properly installed, level, and spaced according to specifications.
  2. Measuring and Cutting: Measure the area and cut the gypsum board to size using a utility knife and straightedge.
  3. Attachment: Attach the board to the framing using appropriate screws or nails, spaced as per recommendations.
  4. Jointing: Apply joint compound to the seams and screw heads, embed joint tape, and apply additional layers of compound as needed.
  5. Sanding: Once dry, sand the joints smooth for a seamless finish.
  6. Finishing: Apply primer and paint or other decorative finishes as desired.

For detailed installation instructions and best practices, please refer to our comprehensive installation guide or contact our technical support team.
