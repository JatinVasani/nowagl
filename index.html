ISI Certified Products

Our products meet the highest quality standards and are certified by the Indian Standards Institute.

The ISI mark is a symbol of quality and safety, ensuring that our products comply with the stringent standards set by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS). This certification guarantees:

  • Consistent product quality
  • Rigorous testing and quality control
  • Compliance with national safety standards
  • Regular factory inspections and audits
